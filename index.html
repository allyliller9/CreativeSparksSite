Welcome to Creative Sparks!

Founded and managed since 2021, our camp combines exciting, hands-on STEM and art concepts to inspire the next generation of girls in science, tech, and creativity.

Where curiosity meets creativity, and every girl discovers her inner scientist and artist!

Why Creative Sparks?

Girls Deserve More Representation in STEM

  • Only 28% of the science and engineering workforce is female
  • Girls are 5x less likely than boys to pursue computer science by high school
  • Women hold just 16% of engineering jobs
  • STEM job growth is outpacing other fields by 8.8%, but the gender gap is widening
  • Only 24% of STEM workers are women, despite making up 50% of the college-educated workforce
  • Women earn only 82 cents for every dollar earned by men in STEM fields
  • Girls who lose interest in STEM typically do so between ages 9-14 - our camp's target age range!

The Power of Early Intervention

Summer camps and early STEM exposure make a huge difference!

  • Girls who participate in hands-on STEM activities before age 12 are 3x more likely to pursue STEM careers
  • 83% of girls who attend STEM camps report increased confidence in math and science
  • Early exposure to female STEM role models increases girls' likelihood of pursuing STEM by 42%
  • Girls who experience collaborative, creative STEM projects are 65% more likely to stay engaged through high school

This camp exists to close that gap—and spark confidence, curiosity, and creativity in every girl. When we start early, we create lasting change!
