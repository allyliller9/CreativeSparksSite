Our Mission

We started Creative Sparks in 2021 after realizing how few fun, engaging spaces existed for girls to get involved in STEM. Representation in science and technology is still way too low for women, especially young girls, and we knew we could do something about it. This camp combines our love for STEM with creativity, community, and confidence-building. Our goal is to help every camper see herself as a future scientist, engineer, inventor, artist, or all three!
